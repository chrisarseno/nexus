## 1. Parties

This Corporate / Employer Contributor Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between:

**Christopher R. Arsenault** (“Maintainer”)  
and  
**the entity identified below** (“Employer”).

Employer executes this Agreement to permit its employee(s), contractor(s), or agent(s) (“Contributors”) to submit Contributions to the Project under the terms below.

---

## 2. Purpose

This Agreement ensures that the Maintainer receives the rights necessary to:

- accept Contributions from Employer personnel  
- include them in the **AGPL-3.0 open-source version**  
- include them in the **commercial proprietary version**  
- re-license Contributions in future versions  
- commercialize Contributions without restriction  
- avoid future intellectual property disputes  

---

## 3. Definitions

**“Project”**  
The software, repositories, models, documentation, and related assets maintained by the Maintainer.

**“Contribution”**  
Any original work intentionally submitted by Contributors, including code, models, documentation, tests, or data.

**“Submit”**  
Any pull request, patch, upload, email, or other intentional transmission of work to the Project.

**“Contributor”**  
Any employee, contractor, or agent of Employer who submits Contributions under this Agreement.

---

## 4. Employer Copyright License

Employer grants the Maintainer a **perpetual, worldwide, irrevocable, transferable, sublicensable, royalty-free license** to:

- use  
- reproduce  
- modify  
- create derivative works  
- distribute  
- publicly display  
- publicly perform  
- re-license  
- and commercialize  

all Contributions submitted by its Contributors.

This includes licensing Contributions under:

- AGPL-3.0  
- proprietary commercial licenses  
- or any future licensing terms  

Employer and Contributors retain their own copyrights.

---

## 5. Employer Patent License

Employer grants the Maintainer and Project users a **perpetual, worldwide, irrevocable, non-exclusive, royalty-free patent license** to:

- make  
- use  
- sell  
- offer for sale  
- import  
- and otherwise transfer  

the Contribution and derivative works.

---

## 6. Patent Retaliation Clause

If Employer initiates a patent infringement claim against:

- the Maintainer,  
- any Project user, or  
- any Project contributor  

related to the Contribution or the Project,

**all rights granted under this Agreement terminate immediately.**

---

## 7. Authority and Warranty

Employer warrants that:

1. Contributors are authorized to submit Contributions.
2. Employer holds sufficient rights in all Contributions.
3. Contributions do not knowingly violate third-party rights.
4. No Contribution will include confidential or proprietary information.
5. Employer has the legal authority to enter this Agreement.

---

## 8. No Confidential Information

Employer agrees not to submit trade secrets or confidential information.  
All Contributions are deemed non-confidential and intentionally provided for open/public use.

---

## 9. No Obligation to Merge

The Maintainer is not obligated to incorporate any Contribution.

---

## 10. Indemnification

Employer agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Maintainer from claims arising out of:

- breach of this Agreement  
- copyright or patent infringement  
- unauthorized submissions  

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **State of Florida**, regardless of conflict-of-law rules.

---

## 12. Term and Effect

This Agreement remains in effect until revoked in writing.  
Revocation does not affect rights for Contributions already submitted.
